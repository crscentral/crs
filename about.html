Our Story — We Started with a Simple Observation, and Built a Company Around It

Every independent hotel owner we have ever spoken to faces the same quiet frustration. They know their property has potential. They can see the demand in the market. But somehow, the revenue never quite reflects what they believe their hotel is worth.

The reason, almost universally, is not the property. It is the absence of the revenue management infrastructure that every large hotel chain takes for granted. The Marriotts, Hiltons, and Hyatts of the world do not earn more simply because their hotels are nicer. They earn more because they have dedicated revenue management teams, sophisticated channel distribution tools, and commercial strategies refined over decades. That expertise is baked into their daily operations — and it gives them a structural advantage that independent properties simply cannot match on their own.

CRS Central was founded to change that.

Who We Are

CRS Central is a specialized hospitality revenue management and reservations support company dedicated to one clear purpose: helping independent and mid-scale hotels perform at the same level as the world's leading hotel chains. Operated under CRS Chauhan Private Limited, CRS Central brings together a team of seasoned hospitality professionals and former senior hoteliers with extensive experience in international hotel markets.

Our senior leadership includes individuals who have served as General Managers at five-star properties — which means our strategies are not just data-driven; they are operationally grounded and commercially proven. We believe the playing field between large branded hotels and independent properties is not as uneven as it seems — the gap is primarily one of resources and expertise. CRS Central exists to close that gap.

Our Expertise & Approach

CRS Central works as a strategic revenue partner to hotels, functioning as an extension of the property's own management team. We focus on three core outcomes: stronger pricing strategy, more effective distribution, and improved booking conversion — all while reducing the operational burden on hotel owners and their teams.

By combining market intelligence, revenue analytics, and professional reservations support, we ensure every booking opportunity is optimized and every distribution channel performs at its full potential. Hotels that partner with CRS Central typically experience 10% to 40% improvement in revenue performance, depending on market conditions, current positioning, and operational setup.

Revenue management, as we practice it, is not simply about filling rooms. It is about selling the right room, at the right price, to the right guest, through the right channel — consistently, every single day.

  • Protect and grow room rates while improving occupancy.
  • Strengthen OTA visibility and booking conversion performance.
  • Optimize channel distribution to reduce commission costs.
  • Convert inquiries — whether by phone, OTA, or direct — into confirmed bookings.
  • Improve overall hotel profitability through disciplined revenue strategy.

We are not an outsourced vendor. We are a revenue partner — and that distinction matters in everything we do.

What Makes Us Different

  • We Come From Hotels: Our team has worked as revenue managers, general managers, and operations leaders. We understand hotel realities.
  • We Act as Your Team: We integrate with your operations, respond to market changes, and act on your hotel's behalf every day.
  • We Focus on Net Revenue: We optimize for net profit — what stays in your pocket after commissions and costs.
  • We Are Transparent: Clear reporting, honest communication, and no hidden fees. You always know our actions and logic.
  • We Are Here: Bangkok-based with physical presence in Thailand and Laos. We inspect properties and meet in person.
  • We Are Flexible: We adapt our approach to your scale, whether a 20-room boutique or 200-room resort.

Global Presence

CRS Central currently supports hotels across Thailand, India, Nepal, Laos, and beyond. Our client portfolio spans independent hotels, boutique resorts, and villa properties — particularly those seeking to improve revenue performance and online distribution.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become the most trusted global revenue management partner for independent hotels — enabling them to compete confidently with large hotel chains.

Mission: To empower independent hotels and resorts to increase revenue, optimize operations, and elevate guest satisfaction through brand-aligned solutions.
